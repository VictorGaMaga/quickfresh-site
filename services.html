Our Services

All QuickFresh treatments use Australian-made enzymatic and premium cleaning solutions designed for safe, residue-free results. Each service includes inspection, pre-treatment and hot-water or mechanical cleaning as appropriate.

Carpet Steam Cleaning

Deep steam extraction using an enzymatic pre-treatment. Removes embedded soil, odours and allergens for a refreshed finish.

Commercial Dry Carpet Cleaning

Low-moisture encapsulation process for offices and retail spaces. Fast drying and ideal for large open areas. From $3 / m².

Upholstery Cleaning

Steam cleaning for sofas, armchairs and dining chairs with enzymatic fabric care. Safe for most fibres and allergy-friendly.

Mattress Cleaning

Steam extraction with an enzymatic hygiene treatment. Neutralises odours, bacteria and allergens for a healthier sleep.

Rug Cleaning (on-site)

Controlled hot-water extraction using gentle enzymatic pre-treatment. Suitable for most synthetic and low-risk rugs.

Tile & Grout Cleaning

Premium tile solution with mechanical scrubbing and rinse. Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms and open areas. From $8 / m² · Min $200.

Stain & Odour Treatments

Targeted premium solutions for coffee, wine and pet stains. Most stains improve significantly; some may remain lightened.

Flood & Water Extraction

Emergency extraction and drying support using truck-mount or portable systems for fast response.

Notes

  • Final pricing confirmed on site after inspection of access, stains and materials.
  • Minimum call-out $149 (tile cleaning · $200 min).

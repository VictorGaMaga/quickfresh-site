Our Cleaning Services

We use Australian-made enzymatic and premium solutions with hot-water extraction (steam) or low-moisture systems as appropriate. Every job includes inspection, targeted pre-treatment and a professional rinse for low-residue results.

Carpet Steam Cleaning

Deep hot-water extraction with enzymatic pre-spray to lift soil, odours and allergens.

  • Truck-mount or high-performance portable
  • Targeted stain spotting included
  • Fast airflow drying on completion

Commercial Low-Moisture (Encap)

Low-moisture encapsulation for offices and retail. Rapid dry and minimal disruption.

  • Ideal for large open areas
  • After-hours scheduling available
  • From $3 / m² · Quotes on site

Upholstery Cleaning

Careful fabric cleaning for sofas, armchairs and dining chairs — enzyme pre-treat & steam rinse.

  • Suitable for most synthetics and blends
  • Delicate process for cushions & seams
  • Optional fabric protection (Scotchgard)

Mattress Cleaning

Hygienic hot-water extraction plus enzyme treatment to neutralise odour, bacteria and allergens.

  • Top or both sides as requested
  • Suitable for most modern mattresses
  • Optional anti-mite & protector add-ons

Rug Cleaning (On-site)

Controlled extraction with gentle enzymatic pre-treat. For synthetic and low-risk rugs.

  • Colourfastness check before cleaning
  • Careful temperature & moisture control
  • Off-site wash available for sensitive rugs

Tile & Grout Cleaning

Professional tile solution with mechanical agitation and hot rinse — kitchens, bathrooms and living areas.

  • Grout line focus and edges detailed
  • Neutralised, low-residue finish
  • From $8 / m² · Min $200

Stain & Odour Treatments

Targeted processes for coffee, wine and pet issues. Honest expectations on what can be improved.

  • Specialist spotting chemistry
  • Odour neutralisation as required
  • Some stains may remain lightly visible

Flood & Water Extraction

Emergency water removal with truck-mount/portable systems and airflow drying support.

  • Moisture checks and basic sanitise
  • Access and contents assessed on arrival
  • Insurance-friendly invoicing available
